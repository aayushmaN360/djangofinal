About Sanity Check

Creating a safer, more positive internet one post at a time.

Our Mission

Sanity Check is dedicated to fostering healthy online discussions and combating digital toxicity. We believe in creating a platform where ideas can be shared constructively and communities can thrive without harmful behavior.

What We Do

We focus on providing clear resources and fostering a strong community through:

  • Clear, fair guidelines that promote healthy discussion.
  • Educational resources and tips for digital citizenship.
  • Safe spaces for vulnerable communities to connect.

Our Team

We're a passionate group of developers, community managers, and advocates who believe in making the internet a better place. Our team combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of online community dynamics.

Our Core Values

Respect

Treating every member with dignity and understanding.

Safety

Creating secure spaces free from harassment and harm.

Inclusion

Welcoming diverse perspectives and backgrounds.
